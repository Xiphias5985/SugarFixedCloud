How much is ok?

The World Health Organization (WHO) recommends you consume no more than 50 grams of sugar per day.

A sweet by any other name.....

The list below contains some of the most common sugars.

  • Brown sugar
  • Corn sweetener
  • Corn syrup
  • Fruit juice concentrates
  • High-fructose corn syrup
  • Honey
  • Invert sugar
  • Malt sugar
  • Molasses
  • Syrup sugar molecules ending in "ose" (dextrose, fructose, glucose, lactose, maltose, sucrose)

Is there a nutritional difference between the types of sugars?

In short, no. They have taste different due to varying processing techniques but are all 100% carbohydrate.
